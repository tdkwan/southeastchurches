Lord's Move to Europe

Support for the saints from the Southeast churches


This page is to facilitate the giving of the saints in the Southeast churches, specifically to support those saints burdened to serve and who are serving in Europe and sent from the Southeast churches.

Burdened saints who would like to participate in the fellowship unto the furtherance of the gospel (Phil 1:5) may use set up a "recurring payment" option with Zelle. Our Zelle account is: southeastchurches@gmail.com. Zelle is often available through major banking institutions. We strongly encourage using Zelle and using the "recurring payment" option as it is FREE of any fees. The recommended recurring giving amount is $35/month per giving unit.
