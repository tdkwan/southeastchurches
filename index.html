Lord's Move to Europe - Germany

Support for the saints from the Southeast churches


This page is to facilitate the giving of the saints in the Southeast churches, specifically to support those saints burdened to serve and who are serving in Europe and Germany sent from the Southeast churches.

Burdened saints who would like to participate in the fellowship unto the furtherance of the gospel (Phil 1:5) may use this site to give offerings by ACH (checking account) and credit cards (though we strongly encourage the use of ACH, with lower fees).



Philippians 1:5
For your fellowship unto the furtherance of the gospel from the first day until now;

Philippians 4:15-16
And you yourselves also know, Philippians, that in the beginning of the gospel, when I went out from Macedonia, no church had fellowship with me in the account of giving and receiving except you only; For even in Thessalonica you sent both once and again to my need.

"We need to see how to carry out the spread of the Lord's recovery in the countries of Europe. Our going to Europe should be neither by a work nor by a movement but by emigration."
(The World Situation and the Direction of the Lord's Move, p. 47)
